Bean Juice Cafe

Located in Eau Claire, Bean Juice Café gives off cozy and earthy vibes. This vegan-friendly coffee bar focuses on providing an assortment of sustainable and free-trade coffee and tea-based beverages for our customers. The bakers rise early to ensure we have the freshest baked goods for you to enjoy everyday. We cannot wait for you to come visit us whether it’s for a coffee date, business meeting or a study session!
